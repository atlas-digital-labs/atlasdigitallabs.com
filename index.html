Whiskey Atlas

A whiskey companion app designed to help users organize bottles, track tastings, explore brands, follow releases, and build a smarter personal collection.

What we are building

Collection Tools

A simple way to save bottles, organize a personal shelf, and keep track of what is owned, opened, finished, or on the wish list.

Tasting Notes

Structured tasting flows for aroma, palate, finish, value, ratings, and notes that make whiskey tracking easier and more consistent.

Discovery

Brand pages, release details, regional exploration, and whiskey data designed to help users find bottles they actually care about.
